Dal Fry

Description

Dal fry is a popular Indian lentil dish where soft cooked toor dal (Skinned Split pigeon peas) aka lentils are simmered with a fried tempering consisting of ghee, whole and ground spices, onions, tomatoes and herbs. There is actually no deep frying involved in the process of making dal fry, but the tempering is fried in 1 to 2 tablespoons ghee or oil to bring out the aroma of various aromatic ingredients.

Ingredients
  • ¾ cup toor dal
  • 3½ cup water
  • 1 tbsp ghee / clarified butter
    • <1i>tsp mustard / rai <1i>1 tsp jeera / cumin <1i>1 dried red chilli (broken) few curry leaves <1i>pinch of hing / asafoetida <1i>1 onion (finely chopped) <1i> 1 tsp ginger garlic paste <1i> 1 green chilli (slit) <1i> ¼ tsp turmeric / haldi <1i> ½ tsp kashmiri red chilli powder / lal mirch powder <1i> ½ tsp coriander powder <1i> 1 tomato (finely chopped) <1i> 1 tsp salt <1i> ¼ tsp garam masala <1i> 1 tbsp coriander (finely chopped) <1i> 1 tsp kasuri methi / dry fenugreek leaves
Steps
  1. Firstly in a pressure cook ¾ cup toor dal and 3 cup water for 5 whistles.
  2. Now in another kadai heat 1 tbsp ghee and splutter 1 tsp mustard, 1 tsp jeera, 1 dried red chilli, few curry leaves and pinch of hing.
  3. Add in 1 onion followed by 1 tsp ginger garlic paste and 1 green chilli. saute well.
  4. Further add ¼ tsp turmeric, ½ tsp chilli powder and ½ tsp coriander powder. saute on low flame.
  5. Additionally add 1 tomato and saute till it turns soft and mushy.
  6. Add the cooked dal along with 1 tsp salt. mix well.
  7. Simmer for 5 minutes or till dal turns smooth and absorbs flavour.
  8. Now add ¼ tsp garam masala, 1 tbsp coriander and 1 tsp crushed kasuri methi.
  9. Finally, serve dal fry with hot jeera rice or ghee rice.
